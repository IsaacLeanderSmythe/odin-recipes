Maggi 5-min noodles

Description

Maggi is an international brand of seasonings, instant soups, and noodles that originated in Switzerland in the late 19th century. The Maggi company was acquired by Nestl√© in 1947

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Noodles
  • Maggi masala

Steps

  1. Boil some water
  2. Place the maggi noodles in the boiling water and put the stove on a simmer
  3. Wait for 2 minutes
  4. Empty the contents of the Masala sachet into the Noodles
  5. Stir to even out the masala
  6. Cook for 3 mins and Switch off
