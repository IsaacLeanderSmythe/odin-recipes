Vegetable Macaroni

Description

Macaroni with vegetables is a wonderful meal to have either in the morning or in the evening. Filled with crunchy veggies, mixed with some spices and sauces, this recipe will go on to your refrigerator!

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Carrot
  • Peas
  • French beans
  • Capsicum
  • Cauliflower
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Onions leaks
  • Tomato sauce
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Ground spices of choice
  • Chillies

Steps

  1. Boil some water
  2. Place the macaroni in the water once it starts boiling
  3. Take a wok and season with oil
  4. Throw in some diced onions and chillies
  5. Saute for some time
  6. Add the diced up tomatoes
  7. Wait for about 5 mins for the tomatoes to get smashed
  8. Add the diced veggies
  9. Once the macaroni turns soft strain out the water
  10. Add the necessary amount of salt, sauce, herbs and spices
  11. Add in the macaroni and mix the content fr uniformity
  12. Serve hot and enjoy
